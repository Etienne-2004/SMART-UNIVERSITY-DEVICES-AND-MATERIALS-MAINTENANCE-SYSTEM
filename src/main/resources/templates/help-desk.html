System Training & User Manual
1. Account Activation Workflow

Every user (except Global Admins) follows this lifecycle to ensure national security:

  • Registration: Complete the form with your University/College details.
  • Email Verification: Enter the OTP sent to your email to prove ownership.
  • Admin Approval: Your account enters "PENDING_APPROVAL". A campus admin must verify your staff/student ID.
  • Activation: Once approved, you gain full access to your specific dashboard.
2. Asset Coding Standard (RP-International Pattern)

Use the following hierarchy for all new registrations:

INSTITUTION / BLOCK / ROOM / ASSET_TYPE + ID

Example: RPKAR/HOSP/LAB01/PC05 (RP Karongi, Hospitality, Lab 01, PC #5)
