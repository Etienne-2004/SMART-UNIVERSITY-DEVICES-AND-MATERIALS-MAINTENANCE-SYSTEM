Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 14, 2026

1. Information Collection

Smart University MMS collects personal information such as full name, email address, university affiliation, and phone number upon registration. This data is essential for maintaining accountability in device and material management.

2. Data Usage

Your information is used to personalize your dashboard, facilitate communication regarding maintenance requests, and ensure system security via Two-Factor Authentication (OTP). We do not share your data with external third parties without administrative consent.

3. Security Protocals

We implement high-level encryption for all user passwords (BCrypt) and secure all API interactions. Multi-Factor Authentication is enabled by default for all users to prevent unauthorized access to the national maintenance grid.

4. Contact

For any inquiries regarding your data, contact the system administrator at support@smartuni.rw.
