# Smart University Device & Material Naming Convention Guide

Welcome to the **Smart University Material & Device Naming Convention Guide**! This document explains the standardized format used across all universities and colleges in Rwanda for reporting devices and materials in the system.

## 📌 The Standardization Format

To ensure every piece of equipment is easily locatable and uniquely identifiable, all devices and materials **must** follow this naming syntax when being reported by the staff or cleaners:

`[UNIVERSITY_ABBR] / [BLOCK_OR_LAB] / [ITEM_TYPE][ID_NUMBER]`

---

### 1️⃣ Breakdown of the Sections

#### A. `[UNIVERSITY_ABBR]`
The official abbreviation of the University or College where the item is located.
*Example: `RPKAR` for RP Karongi College, `CST` for College of Science and Technology.*

#### B. `[BLOCK_OR_LAB]`
The abbreviation or short name of the specific block, room, or laboratory.
*Examples:*
* `HOSP` = Hospitality Block
* `ICT LAB01` = ICT Laboratory 1
* `MEE` = Mechanical Block
* `ADM` = Administration Block
* `LIB 01` = Library 01
* `ST AF` = Student Affairs
* `FIN` = Finance
* `MH` = Main Hall
* `CA CE` = Career Center
* `GA` = Gate
* `EEE LAB` = Electrical Engineering Lab
* `TOI` = Toilet (can be combined with other blocks, e.g., `HOSP/TOI`)

#### C. `[ITEM_TYPE]` + `[ID_NUMBER]`
A 2-4 letter abbreviation of the material or device type, followed immediately by its unique asset number (typically 2 or 3 digits).
*Examples:*
* `SO01` = Socket 01
* `TB01` = Table 01
* `PC01` = Personal Computer 01
* `TANK01` = Tank 01
* `BU01` = Bulb 01
* `MI01` = Mirror 01
* `BK ST010` = Book Stand 010 (can contain spaces if necessary)
* `CH01` = Chair 01
* `DO01` = Door 01
* `SW05` = Switch 05
* `PR01` = Printer 01
* `CAM01` = Camera 01
* `MU01` = Multimeter 01

---

## 🛠️ Examples of Reporting

Now, when you use the dashboard to report an issue, the **Room / Location** or **Asset ID** field should combine these elements. Here are practical examples based on the RP Karongi College standard:

### Devices:
* **RPKAR/HOSP/F&B/SO01** = RP Karongi College, Hospitality block, Food & Beverage room, socket 01.
* **RPKAR/HOSP/PC01** = RP Karongi College, Hospitality block, personal computer 01.
* **RPKAR/MEE/PC02** = RP Karongi College, Mechanical block, personal computer 02.
* **RPKAR/ADM/BU01** = RP Karongi College, Administration block, bulb 01.
* **RPKAR/CO LA01/SW05** = RP Karongi College, Computer lab 01, switch 05.
* **RPKAR/CA CE/PR01** = RP Karongi College, Career center, printer 01.
* **RPKAR/GA/CAM01** = RP Karongi College, Gate, camera 01.
* **RPKAR/EEE LAB/MU01** = RP Karongi College, Electrical engineering lab, multimeter 01.

### Materials:
* **RPKAR/ICT LAB01/TB01** = RP Karongi College, ICT lab 01, table 01.
* **RPKAR/HOSP/TANK01** = RP Karongi College, Hospitality block, tank 01.
* **RPKAR/HOSP/TOI/MI01** = RP Karongi College, Hospitality block, toilet, mirror 01.
* **RPKAR/LIB 01/BK ST010** = RP Karongi College, Library 01, book stand 010.
* **RPKAR/ST AF/CH01** = RP Karongi College, Student affair, chair 01.
* **RPKAR/FIN/DO01** = RP Karongi College, Finance, door 01.
* **RPKAR/MH/CH04** = RP Karongi College, Main hall, chair 04.

## ⚠️ Important Note for Users
When you use the "Report Damage" or "Register Device" panel:
1. Choose the actual Category (e.g. `TABLE`, `LAPTOP`, `DOOR`).
2. Type exactly this standardized string into the **"Room / Location"** or **"Device ID"** input box.
3. This allows Technicians and Admins to pinpoint the exact item instantly across any university campus.
